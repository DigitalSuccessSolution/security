Rudra Group Security Pvt. Ltd. (RGSPL) is one of the leading security service providers in India. With over 15 years of experience and a highly trained staff, RGSPL was founded in 2006 and is among the first security agencies in India to be awarded the ISO 9001:2015 certification.

We offer security solutions for Event Management, Retail Management, Electronic Surveillance, K9 Squads, Cash Vans, and more. Our expert team is equipped to handle all your security needs with professionalism and efficiency.

Our team of qualified security professionals is committed to delivering top-tier security services, ensuring peace of mind and industry-leading standards.

Our Mission

As the leading security service provider in India. our aim is to provide the best security service to our customers. Our main focus is on clear and concise communication which enables us to protect your assets and loved ones round the clock. We combine the on-field experience of our seasoned experts and advanced technology to strive towards perfection across all security aspects. Our ethics, morals, time commitment and afford ability makes us stand out from the crowd.
